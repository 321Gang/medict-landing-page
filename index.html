Oncology & Radiomics Research

OnkoDICOM

OnkoDICOM was created with Radiation Oncologists to allow Radiation Oncologists to do research on DICOM standard image sets

What OnkoDICOM offers

3D RECONSTRUCTION OF MEDICAL IMAGES

View and interact with DICOM standard medical images in all three anatomical planes, as well as a reconstructed 3D model

REGION OF INTEREST CREATION

Fast, easy, and intuitive creation of region of interest, which defines structures of a scan

REGION OF INTEREST MANIPULATION

Perform geometrical operation (Union, Intersection, Difference, Scaling and Ring Creation) on Region of Interest to visualise hypothetical medical situation or to create dataset for radiomics research

TRANSFER REGION OF INTEREST

Working on information from multiple scans by transferring Region of Interest from one image set to another
